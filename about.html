Contributions and description

jUCMNav was started as a capstone project for the software engineering bachelor's degree at the University of Ottawa by Jean-Philippe Daigle, Jason Kealey, Jordan McManus, and Etienne Tremblay.

Developed under the supervision of Daniel Amyot, jUCMNav intends to replace UCMNav, the original Use Case Map navigator.

Since then, it has been extended to support the full User Requirements Notation (URN), including the Goal-oriented Requirement Language (GRL).

Many analysis and transformation tools have also been added, and more are being developed!

For more information about Use Case Maps, please visit usecasemaps.org.

Some graphical resources designed by and released under the EPL under permission from Steve Hutchison.

The support for the GRL notation has been provided by Jean-François Roy in the course of his Master's thesis.

The support for UCM scenarios has been provided by Jason Kealey in the course of his Master's thesis.

Many thanks to Gunter Mussbacher for continued help since the project's start.
