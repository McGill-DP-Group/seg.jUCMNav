ConnectionAnchors used by the figures and possibly by the UCMConnectionRouter.

Package Specification

EditParts define source and target connection anchors. GEF uses these to have handles to which connections may be tied. We've circumvented this mechanisms for our spline drawing but our AndFork/AndJoin figures use connection anchors for all of the outgoing/incoming branches.
